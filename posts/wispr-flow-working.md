I've experimented with several different speech-to-task systems for use with my terminal. Currently I'm using Ghost TTY. It's working well in conjunction with Wispr Flow. There might be other combinations that work well or could work well, but after experimenting and not being happy with anything, I'm adopting this for now. This is a big breakthrough for me and I'm impressed with the ability of Wispr Flow to give reasonable editing to my kind of broken English.
